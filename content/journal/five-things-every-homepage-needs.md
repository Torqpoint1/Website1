Most homepages try to say everything and end up saying nothing. A homepage that converts is disciplined. Here are the five elements that earn their place.

## 1. A headline that says what you do

No riddles. A visitor should understand your offer and who it’s for in one short line.

## 2. A single, obvious next step

One primary action, repeated. “Get a quote” or “Book a call” — not five competing buttons.

## 3. Proof you can be trusted

Reviews, recognisable clients, real photos of real work. Borrowed credibility lowers the visitor’s guard.

## 4. The answer to “why you?”

A short, specific reason you’re the safe choice — not “quality service and great prices,” which everyone claims.

## 5. The basics, findable in a second

- Where you’re based and the areas you cover
- How to get in touch, visible without scrolling
- What you actually offer, in plain terms

Strip away anything that doesn’t serve those five. Clarity is what converts — not clutter.

When someone searches for a service near them, the map results and the box on the right of the screen usually get seen before anything else. That box is your Google Business Profile — free to claim, and one of the highest-return hours you can spend on marketing.

## The basics most people miss

- Correct categories — this is how Google knows what to show you for
- Accurate service areas, not just an address
- Real photos of your work, updated now and then
- Opening hours that are actually right

## Reviews are the engine

A steady flow of recent reviews does two things: it pushes you up the local results, and it reassures the person deciding. A profile with forty reviews from this year beats one with five from 2022. Ask every happy client.

## Post like it’s a mini feed

You can post updates, offers, and photos straight to your profile. Few businesses bother, which is exactly why doing it sets you apart. It signals to Google — and to searchers — that you’re active and open for business.

None of this costs a penny. It just costs attention, which is why the businesses that give it theirs quietly pull ahead.

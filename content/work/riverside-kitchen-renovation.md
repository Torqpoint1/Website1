## The brief

Cotswold Interiors had just completed one of their best kitchens to date — a full renovation for a family in Bourton-on-the-Water, combining hand-painted cabinetry with Silestone worktops and bespoke oak detailing.

The photos were stunning. They were sitting in a phone gallery.

## What we did

We took the raw images and spent time understanding the project — the brief, the decisions made, the problems solved. Then we built out a full content package:

- A long-form case study optimised for local search
- Three social posts timed across the month
- A newsletter feature that went to their 400-strong subscriber list

## The result

Within six weeks the case study was ranking on page one for "kitchen renovation Bourton-on-the-Water." The client had two enquiries directly reference the article in their first call.

The photos finally did the job they deserved to do.
